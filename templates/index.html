Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (ALL)

Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia (ALL) is a fast-progressing blood cancer that affects white blood cells. It commonly impacts children and young adults but can occur at any age. The disease disrupts normal blood cell production, leading to symptoms like fatigue, infections, and bleeding. Early diagnosis and stage classification are critical for timely and effective treatment.

Model Information

Our deep learning model classifies ALL into distinct stages—Benign, Early, Pre, and Pro—helping healthcare professionals make informed decisions. This tool demonstrates the power of AI in enhancing diagnostic accuracy and saving lives.

Performance Metrics:

  • Overall Accuracy: 98.16%
  • Benign: Precision 96%, Recall 94.74%
  • Early: Precision 97.28%, Recall 96.62%
  • Pre: Precision 100%, Recall 100%
  • Pro: Precision 98.37%, Recall 100%
